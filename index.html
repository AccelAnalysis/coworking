Service Pillar

Member Pipeline Radar™

Instead of hoping the right people stumble across your space, Member Pipeline Radar helps you see who is nearby, what they need, and how to reach them.

  • Profile Definition: We clarify the member profiles that are healthiest for your space (solo professionals, micro-teams, creators, etc.).
  • Local Mapping: We map businesses, home-based workers, and organizations within a practical radius of your location.
  • Priority Tiers: Prospects are grouped into tiers so you can layer your outreach and offers intelligently.
  • Cadence Design: Light, repeatable touchpoints designed to warm interest over time, not overwhelm people in week one.

Deliverable: a structured view of your “next 6–18 months of members” and a plan to consistently invite them into your space.

There are clusters of home-based businesses, remote workers, and target companies around your space.
Service Pillar

Location & Expansion Strategy

Thinking about opening or repositioning a location? We help you evaluate where your concept fits best and how to communicate that value to the right audience.

  • Market Scans: Evaluate potential neighborhoods or cities using business density, demographics, and competitor presence.
  • Concept Fit: Align your membership mix and pricing with the needs of local businesses and workers.
  • Launch & Repositioning: Design pre-launch and post-launch outreach tailored to each location.
  • Feedback Loops: Pair utilization and membership data with what you see on the ground to refine over time.

Result: more confidence that your locations and offerings are matched to real-world demand, not guesses or fads.
